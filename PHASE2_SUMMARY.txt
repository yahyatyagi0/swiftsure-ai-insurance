## 🧠 AI Intelligence

### Risk Formula
```
Risk = (Activity × 0.5) + (Claims × 0.3) + (Fraud × 0.2)

Range: 0-100
- 0-30: Low Risk → $120/mo
- 30-60: Medium Risk → $250/mo
- 60-100: High Risk → $450/mo
```

### Claim Decision Logic
```
if fraud_risk > 65 OR amount > $50k:
    decision = "Rejected"
elif fraud_risk > 30:
    decision = "Under Review"
else:
    decision = "Approved"
```

### Recommendations System
```
Dynamic pool of 4 recommendations
Random selection of 3 per session
Contextual to risk level
Updates on refresh
```
